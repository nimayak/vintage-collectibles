Our First Collection

Introduction

We found these Adventure Time playing cards in an op-shop down-town one lazy sunday afternoon, and the bright colours and interesting artwork caught our eyes. The collection features 8 playing cards with 6 different roles which includes; lady, companion, princess, wizard, 2 heros and 2 gossips. We liked these cards because we are fans of the TV show ‘Adventure Time’ and thought these cards would be prized possessions to any true fan.

The Lady Card

Description:
The Lady card is worth 7 points in the game and is represented by the character Lady Rainicorn. Lady Rainicorn is Princess Bubblegum’s beloved companion and royal stead. She is a rainbow-coloured unicorn with long yellow mane. Her abilities include flying and the ability to change the colour of things at will. She’s fluent in Korean and is described as gentle in nature. This card is very special and is very sought after by die-hard fans.

The Companion Card

Description:
The Companion card is worth 4 points in the game and depicts the character, BMO VER. 2600 (Beemo). Beemo serves as Jack and Finn’s living video game console system and has many functionalities that assist Jack and Finn on their many adventures. Beemo has a jade green traditional video game controller appearance. Beemo is described as a loyal, trustworthy and helpful companion to Jack and Finn.

The Princess Card

Description:
The Princess card is worth 8 points (the most in the game) and features Princess Bonnibel Bubblegum as the representative. Princess Bubblegum is the 19-year-old ruler of the Candy Kingdom, as well as a scientist and inventor. She has light-pink skin, long pink hair and is usually seen in a hot pink coloured frock. She is described as a fierce, loyal ruler of her kingdom with a strong set of morals. This card would also be very sought after by Princess Bubblegum fans.

The Wizard Card

Description:
The Wizard card is worth 6 points in the game and depicts the character, the Ice King. The Ice King (Simon Petrikov) is the king of the Ice Kingdom and the main villain of the series. He has made numerous attempts to kidnap Princess Bubblegum and force her to marry him, all of which have been thwarted by Jake and Finn. He is an elderly man with light-blue coloured skin and wears a dark-blue wizard’s robe and a gold crown.

The First Hero Card

Description:
This Hero card is worth 5 points in the game and features the character, Finn the Human. Finn Mertens is a 12-year-old human boy who is kind, selfless and believes in justice. His best friend is Jake the Dog; they both go on many adventures throughout the series. He wears a sky-blue t-shirt, denim shorts, green backpack and his signature white hat which covers his whole head except his face.

The Second Hero Card

Description:
This Hero card is worth 5 points in the game and features the character, Jake the Dog. Jake is dog/shape-shifter hybrid and best friend of Finn the Human. He has ‘stretchy powers’ that allow him to change the shape of his body. He is medium-sized bulldog with yellow-orange skin. He is described as laid-back, humorous and worry-free.

The First Gossip Card

Description:
This Gossip card is worth 3 points and is represented by the character, Marceline Abadeer. Marceline The Vampire Queen is a young half-demon/half-human who is known to join Finn, Jake and Princess Bubblegum on their adventures. She is fearless, humorous and mischievous at times. She has light-grey skin, a slender physique and black hair that reaches the ground and is often seen levitating.

The Second Gossip Card

Description:
This Gossip card is worth 3 points and depicts the character, Lumpy Space Princess. Lumpy Space Princess is the 14-year-old princess of Lumpy Space. She is known for her dramatic, sassy attitude, purple cloud-like appearance and deep nasal voice. She has the ability to float in the air and can sometimes alter her body shape.
